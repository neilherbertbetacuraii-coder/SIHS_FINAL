How to Apply for Scholarships

Application Requirements
  • Completed admission application
  • Academic transcripts and test scores
  • Two teacher recommendation letters
  • Personal essay (500 words)
  • Extracurricular activity documentation
  • Financial aid application (if applying for need-based aid)

Essay Prompt: "Describe how you have demonstrated leadership or made a positive impact in your school or community. What challenges did you face, and what did you learn from the experience?"

Important Notes
  • All scholarships are renewable
  • Maintain 3.0 GPA to retain award
  • Separate application required
  • Early application recommended
  • Deadlines are firm
