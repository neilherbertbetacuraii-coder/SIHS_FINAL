Admission FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about admission process

For Kindergarten, children should be 3 years old by the end of June. For elementary, students must have completed kindergarten or be at least 6 years old. Age proofs through birth certificate are mandatory.

Required documents include: Birth Certificate, Vaccination Records, Previous School Report Card, Medical Certificate, and Parents' ID proof. A complete checklist will be provided during the application process.

Yes, entrance examinations are conducted for classes 2 and above. For Kindergarten and Grade 1, we conduct a readiness assessment and a parent interview.

The complete admission process typically takes 2-3 weeks from application to final admission decision. Applications are processed on a rolling basis throughout the year.

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including monthly, quarterly, and annual payment plans. Special arrangements can be made upon application and discussion with the administration.
