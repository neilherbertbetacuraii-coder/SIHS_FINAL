Holistic Admission Process

We look beyond grades and test scores to find students who will thrive in our community. Our admission requirements ensure we build a diverse and talented student body.

Elementary (Grades 1-5)

Academic Requirements
  • Age appropriate for grade level
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Basic readiness assessment
  • Parent interview
Health & Safety
  • Complete immunization records
  • Physical examination (within 1 year)
  • Dental examination (within 1 year)
  • Vision screening

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Academic Requirements
  • Strong elementary school record
  • Teacher recommendations (2)
  • Math and English placement assessment
  • Student interview
Additional Requirements
  • Complete immunization records
  • Physical examination (within 1 year)
  • Discipline/behavior records
  • Extracurricular activities summary

High School (Grades 9-12)

Academic Requirements
  • Minimum GPA: 3.0 (unweighted)
  • Strong middle school record
  • Standardized test scores (optional)
  • Teacher/counselor recommendations (2)
Assessment Requirements
  • English proficiency assessment
  • Mathematics placement exam
  • Student interview
  • Writing sample

Universal Requirements for All Applicants

Documentation
  • Birth certificate or passport
  • Proof of residency
  • Parent/guardian identification
  • Emergency contact information
  • Application fee payment ($150)
Health & Safety
  • Complete immunization records
  • Physical examination report
  • Health insurance information
  • Allergy and medical conditions
  • Medication administration forms

English Language Proficiency

For Non-Native English Speakers

Students whose primary language is not English may be required to demonstrate English proficiency through one of the following:

  • TOEFL iBT score of 80 or higher
  • IELTS score of 6.5 or higher
  • Duolingo English Test score of 110 or higher
  • WIDA ACCESS test (for current students)
  • Internal English assessment (arranged by school)
ESL Support Available

We offer comprehensive English as a Second Language support for students who need additional language development.

Additional Requirements for Transfer Students

Academic Records
  • Official transcripts from all previous schools
  • Course descriptions and syllabi
  • Grade reports and progress reports
  • Standardized test scores (if available)
  • Academic awards and honors
Conduct & Records
  • Discipline records and incident reports
  • Attendance records
  • Transfer certificate/letter
  • Counselor recommendation
  • Extracurricular activities documentation
Credit Transfer Policy

Transfer credits are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Our academic counselors will review your previous coursework to determine appropriate placement and credit transfer.

Special Considerations & Accommodations

Learning Differences

Students with documented learning differences or disabilities are welcome to apply. We provide comprehensive support services.

  • IEP (Individualized Education Program)
  • 504 Plan documentation
  • Psychological evaluations
  • Accommodations history
Advanced Learners

Gifted and talented students receive additional documentation requirements to ensure appropriate academic placement.

  • IQ test results
  • Achievement test scores
  • Portfolio of work
  • Teacher recommendations

Questions About Requirements?

Our admissions team is here to help you understand our requirements and guide you through the application process.
