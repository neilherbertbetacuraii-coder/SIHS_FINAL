Program Overview

We offer a complete spectrum of educational programs from kindergarten through senior high school, each meticulously designed to meet the developmental needs of our students.

Kindergarten (Ages 3-5)

Our kindergarten program focuses on play-based learning with emphasis on social development, early literacy, and numeracy skills. Children develop in a nurturing environment that encourages creativity and exploration.

Elementary (Grades 1-6)

The elementary curriculum provides a strong foundation in English, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies, augmented with arts, physical education, and technology education.

Junior High (Grades 7-9)

Our junior high program introduces students to specialized subjects while maintaining a broad curriculum base. We emphasize critical thinking, research skills, and character development.

Senior High (Grades 10-12)

The senior high program prepares students for college and careers with advanced academics, specialized tracks, and mentoring. We offer STEM, Arts, and Business-focused programs.
