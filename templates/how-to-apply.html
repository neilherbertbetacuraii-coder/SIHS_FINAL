New Student Applications

Required Documents
  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Academic transcripts (last 2 years)
  • Teacher recommendation letters (2)
  • Medical records and immunization proof
  • Parent/guardian identification
  • Application fee payment
Application Fee

$150 (non-refundable, covers processing costs)

Transfer Student Applications

Required Documents
  • Completed application form
  • Official transcripts from current school
  • Transfer certificate/letter
  • Academic records (last 3 years)
  • Discipline/behavior records
  • Teacher/counselor recommendations (2)
  • Medical records and immunization proof
Transfer Credit Evaluation

Previous coursework evaluated for appropriate placement
